Bandeja Paisa

Description

Bandeja Paisa is Colombia's national dish, a hearty platter that originated in the Antioquia region. This filling combination of beans, rice, meats, and other ingredients represents the rich culinary heritage of Colombia.

Ingredients

  • Red beans
  • White rice
  • Ground beef
  • Chicharrón (fried pork belly)
  • Colombian chorizo
  • Fried egg
  • Plantains
  • Avocado
  • Arepa
  • Hogao sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the red beans until tender
  2. Prepare white rice
  3. Cook ground beef with seasonings
  4. Fry the chicharrón until crispy
  5. Grill the chorizo
  6. Fry the eggs sunny-side up
  7. Fry plantains until golden
  8. Prepare hogao sauce
  9. Warm arepas
  10. Assemble all components on a large plate
  11. Serve with sliced avocado and hogao sauce
